Overview

Purpose

At Lorem Ipsum Whitewater Rafting, we want to make whitewater rafting accessible to everyone- not just elite adrenaline junkies and thrill-seekers. We seek to create adventures and outings that the whole family can enjoy- to foster bonds and relationships among the people we love the most. We explore beautiful nature and provide wonderful experiences so that the memories made will indeed last a lifetime.

Audience

At Lorem Ipsum Whitewater Rafting, our target audience is families- people who come from everyday walks of life and who are looking to make memories. Ideally, we'd look for ages 5-105 and be able to accommodate a broad range of abilities and comfort levels. We serve a broad base of clients and want to be welcoming of everyone and those with a variety of skill/experience levels. While we can take seasoned rafters on an adventure, they will most likely find ours to be too tame for them- we want families to enjoy the outdoors and feel safe and well-cared for as they do so. Families represent a large demographic, and many companies do not cater their offerings to them- focusing on solo or team activities that are high-octane adventures. We want to do more than provide families with a nice day or a place to have a picnic. Amusement parks are often attractive for families due to a wide variety of interests and experiences- and we believe the ultimate amusement park is the great outdoors. By having an eye-catching and easy-to-navigate website, a solid presence on social media, and an Instagram page where customers can tag and post their images; we hope to engage our target audience in a way that feels familiar but also exciting and relevant to current expectations. Our website will have various sections- what's new, FAQs, testimonials, and about sections so people can find information easily and quickly.
